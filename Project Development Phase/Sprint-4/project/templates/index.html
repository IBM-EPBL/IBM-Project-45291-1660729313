Heart Diseases Prediction

GOALS

The main challenge in today's healthcare is provision of best quality services and effective accurate diagnosis. Even if heart diseases are found as the prime source of death in the world in recent years, they are also the ones that can be controlled and managed effectively. The whole accuracy in management of a disease lies on the proper time of detection of that disease. Our proposed model makes an attempt to detect these heart diseases at early stage to avoid disastrous consequences.
